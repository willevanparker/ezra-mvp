The difference

A long-term home, not a flip.

Ezra represents an acquirer backed by insurance-industry family offices — not private equity. The intent is to hold and grow, which is why so much of what owners worry about stays intact.

  • Keep your brand. Your name and identity stay on the door.
  • Keep your team. Employees are retained, not restructured away.
  • Stay in the seat. Existing management remains and keeps real autonomy.
  • Share the upside. Owners can participate in seller equity.
  • Lose the busywork. Administrative and back-office support included.
  • Patient capital. Long-term ownership, built to grow the agency.
